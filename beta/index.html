Our flagship locations are more than charging stations‚Äîthey're community gathering spaces and adventure headquarters. Featuring 8-12 ultra-fast chargers, full-service amenities, curated retail, gourmet dining options, and comfortable lounge spaces with local art and culture.

Waystation

Waystations: Strategic Stops

Perfectly positioned between Basecamps, Waystations ensure you're never far from a charge. Compact yet premium, these locations deliver fast, reliable charging with essential amenities in scenic locations that make every stop worthwhile.

  • 4-6 high-speed charging stalls (150-350kW)
  • Covered charging canopy with lighting
  • Grab-and-go refreshments & coffee
  • Clean restroom facilities
  • Scenic viewpoints & rest areas
  • 24/7 operation & support

The Hub-Satellite Model

Our network strategy mirrors how travelers actually explore. Basecamps serve as destination anchors‚Äîplaces you plan to visit and linger. Waystations fill the gaps, providing reliable charging every 75-100 miles along major routes. Together, they eliminate range anxiety while elevating every mile of your journey. Whether you're road-tripping cross-country or exploring regional gems, Rangeway keeps you charged, comfortable, and confident.
