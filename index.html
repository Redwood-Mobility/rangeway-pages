When are you opening?

Our first locations debut in 2026 in California, then expanding across Colorado, Hawaii, Nevada, Utah, and Washington. Subscribe to Field Notes for priority notification.

What EVs do you accommodate?

Every location features both CCS and NACS connectors. We welcome all electric vehicles.

How fast is the charging?

Our ultra-fast DC charging typically brings most vehicles from 20% to 80% in 20-30 minutes. Just enough time to enjoy a freshly prepared coffee.

Is the lounge complimentary?

Every charging guest enjoys full access to our climate-controlled lounges, high-speed WiFi, and pristine facilities at no additional charge.
