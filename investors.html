The Vision

The Summit: Where Charging Meets Hospitality

The Summit is the full expression of our hospitality vision. Modular guest Lookouts, a central clubhouse, and 100% off-grid solar EV charging create a destination where the stop itself becomes part of the journey.

Inspired by premium outdoor hospitality brands, the Summit model adds lodging revenue to our proven charging and retail channels, creating a compelling unit economics story for investors. Our first Summit is in development alongside our inaugural Basecamp location.
