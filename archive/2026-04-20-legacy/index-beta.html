Why Rangeway

Charging has a hospitality problem. We're the solution.

The EV charging industry has treated charging as a utility problem. But drivers spend 20 to 40 minutes at a charging location. That's not a transaction. It's an experience. Right now, that experience is broken.

Rangeway is building something different: a premium charging network where every stop is designed around you, not just your vehicle. We think like hotel operators, not utility companies. From driver's lounges to overnight Lookouts at our Summit destinations, every detail is designed with hospitality expertise.

Our Founder

Hospitality DNA Meets EV Passion

Zak Winnick, Founder and CEO, brings over 15 years of experience across hospitality operations and technology. His professional background includes IT leadership and hospitality operations at Wynn Las Vegas, Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas, Grand Hyatt Nashville, and Sensei wellness retreats.

As Community Director of the Bay Area Rivian Club and co-founder of NorCal EVs, Zak combines deep hospitality expertise with firsthand EV community insight. That combination drives Rangeway's mission to transform charging from a logistics burden into a premium experience.
